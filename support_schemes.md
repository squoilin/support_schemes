# EU Renewable Energy Support Schemes (2024-2026)

## Ground-mounted Solar PV

| Country | Scheme / Policy | Source / Reference | Comment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Austria (AT) | Sliding Market Premium via auctions. | [E-Control / EAG](https://www.e-control.at/) | Erneuerbaren-Ausbau-Gesetz (EAG, 2021) established competitive auction-based sliding premiums; E-Control administers. |
| Belgium (Brussels) (BE1) | Green Certificates (Quota). | [Brugel](https://www.brugel.brussels/) | Quota obligation on suppliers with tradable green certificates; very limited ground-mounted capacity in Brussels-Capital Region. |
| Belgium (Flanders) (BE2) | Mixed (phasing out Quotas, moving to direct grants). | [VEKA](https://www.vlaanderen.be/veka) | Quota system being phased out; transition to investment support and targeted tenders for large PV. |
| Belgium (Wallonia) (BE3) | Green Certificates (Quota). | [CWaPE](https://www.cwape.be/) | Multiplier-based system with banded certificate values depending on technology and installation size. |
| Bulgaria (BG) | Sliding Premium via auctions. | [Ministry of Energy / EWRC](https://www.me.government.bg/) | Premium auctions under the Energy from Renewable Sources Act (ERSA/ZEVI); smaller legacy installations may still receive FiTs. |
| Croatia (HR) | Market Premium via auctions. | [HROTE](https://www.hrote.hr/) | Croatian Energy Market Operator (HROTE) runs competitive auctions; premium scheme replaced FiTs from 2016 onwards. |
| Cyprus (CY) | Competitive bidding for FiP. | [CERA](https://www.cera.org.cy/) | Auctions for ground-mounted PV awarded as feed-in premiums by the Cyprus Energy Regulatory Authority. |
| Czech Republic (CZ) | Auction-based Market Premiums. | [ERÚ](https://www.eru.cz/) | Under the Act on Supported Energy Sources (Act 165/2012); co-financed via the Modernisation Fund for larger installations. |
| Denmark (DK) | Two-way CfDs / Technology-neutral tenders. | [Danish Energy Agency](https://ens.dk/) | Technology-neutral tenders with two-way CfD mechanism; generators repay when market prices exceed strike price. |
| Estonia (EE) | Sliding Premium via reverse auctions. | [Elering](https://elering.ee/) | TSO Elering administers renewable energy reverse auctions awarding sliding premiums. |
| Finland (FI) | Merchant / Corporate PPAs. | [Energy Authority](https://energiavirasto.fi/) | No active state support for large-scale PV; premium scheme phased out after 2017; market fully merchant/PPA-driven. |
| France (FR) | Two-way CfDs via competitive tenders. | [CRE](https://www.cre.fr/) | Commission de Régulation de l'Énergie (CRE) runs periodic tenders (appels d'offres); two-way CfD via complément de rémunération. |
| Germany (DE) | Sliding Market Premium (FiP) via auctions. | [BNetzA / EEG 2023](https://www.bundesnetzagentur.de/) | Bundesnetzagentur conducts auctions under EEG 2023; sliding premium on top of wholesale revenue; installations >1 MW must participate in auctions. |
| Greece (GR) | Sliding Feed-in Premium via tenders. | [RAAEY (ex-RAE)](https://www.rae.gr/) | Regulatory Authority for Waste, Energy and Water (renamed from RAE in 2022) conducts competitive auctions. |
| Hungary (HU) | METÁR system (two-way CfD). | [MEKH](https://mekh.hu/) | Mandatory off-take (KÁT) for <0.5 MW; competitive bidding for premium (METÁR-Prémium) for >0.5 MW via two-way CfD. |
| Ireland (IE) | RESS (Two-way CfDs). | [DECC / RESS](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/36d8d2-renewable-electricity-support-scheme-ress/) | Renewable Electricity Support Scheme; competitive auctions for 15-year two-way CfDs; RESS 3 auction held in 2024. |
| Italy (IT) | Two-way CfDs via auctions. | [GSE / FER 1](https://www.gse.it/) | Gestore dei Servizi Energetici (GSE) manages auctions under FER 1 Decree; FER X update expected to revise the framework. |
| Latvia (LV) | Merchant / Market-based. | [Ministry of Economics](https://www.em.gov.lv/) | No active state premium scheme for large PV; market operates on merchant basis. Auction-based support framework under development. |
| Lithuania (LT) | Sliding Premium / CfD via auctions. | [VERT (ex-NERC)](https://www.vert.lt/) | State Energy Regulatory Council (renamed VERT in 2023) conducts auctions; 12-year premium contracts. |
| Luxembourg (LU) | Market Premium via tenders. | [ILR](https://web.ilr.lu/) | Institut Luxembourgeois de Régulation; periodic calls for tenders for systems >500 kWp. |
| Malta (MT) | Feed-in Tariffs via bidding. | [REWS](https://www.rews.org.mt/) | Regulator for Energy and Water Services manages FiT allocations; limited ground-mounted PV capacity on the island. |
| Netherlands (NL) | SDE++ Scheme (Sliding FiP/CfD). | [RVO](https://www.rvo.nl/) | Stimulering Duurzame Energieproductie en Klimaattransitie (SDE++); technology-neutral operating aid with sliding premium base amount. |
| Norway (NO) | Merchant / PPAs. | [NVE](https://www.nve.no/) | No state RES support for solar PV in Norway; market-driven investment via corporate PPAs. |
| Poland (PL) | Two-way CfDs via auctions. | [URE / RES Act](https://www.ure.gov.pl/) | Energy Regulatory Office (URE) administers auctions under the RES Act (2015, amended); 15-year support contracts. |
| Portugal (PT) | CfDs via competitive tenders. | [DGEG](https://www.dgeg.gov.pt/) | Directorate-General for Energy and Geology; large-scale solar tenders, some rounds with negative bidding (generators pay the state). |
| Romania (RO) | Two-way CfDs. | [ANRE](https://www.anre.ro/) | CfD framework under national RES legislation co-financed by the Modernisation Fund; first CfD auctions launched 2023-2024. |
| Slovakia (SK) | Feed-in Premium via auctions. | [ÚRSO](https://www.urso.gov.sk/) | Regulatory Office for Network Industries oversees premium allocation; Modernisation Fund co-financing for larger plants. |
| Slovenia (SI) | Market Premium via tenders. | [Borzen](https://www.borzen.si/) | Borzen (Centre for RES/CHP Support) manages premium auctions and administers the Slovenian RES support scheme. |
| Spain (ES) | REER Pay-as-bid tenders. | [MITECO](https://www.miteco.gob.es/) | Régimen Económico de Energías Renovables (REER); pay-as-bid competitive tenders administered by Ministry for Ecological Transition. |
| Sweden (SE) | Merchant / Corporate PPAs (Elcertifikat phased out). | [Swedish Energy Agency](https://www.energimyndigheten.se) | Elcertifikat scheme closed to new entrants 2021; large-scale PV is entirely merchant or PPA-based. |
| Switzerland (CH) | Mixed: sliding premium + legacy FiT. | [SFOE / Pronovo](https://www.pronovo.ch/) | Legacy KEV feed-in tariff closed to new applicants; transition to auction-based sliding premiums under revised Energy Act (2023). |
| United Kingdom (UK) | Contracts for Difference (CfD). | [LCCC / DESNZ](https://www.lowcarboncontracts.uk/) | Allocation Rounds (AR) managed by Low Carbon Contracts Company; AR6 (2024) included solar PV at significant scale. |

## Rooftop Solar PV

| Country | Scheme / Policy | Source / Reference | Comment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Austria (AT) | Investment Grants. | [Klima- und Energiefonds / EAG](https://www.klimafonds.gv.at/) | One-off investment grants for PV + storage; administered by the Climate and Energy Fund under the EAG framework. |
| Belgium (Brussels) (BE1) | Net Billing & Green Certificates. | [Brugel](https://www.brugel.brussels/) | Green certificates for production + net billing (compteur qui tourne à l'envers replaced by prosumer tariff). |
| Belgium (Flanders) (BE2) | Investment Subsidies & Net Billing. | [VEKA](https://www.vlaanderen.be/veka) | Digital meter rollout completed; net billing in place. Investment premiums shrinking as grid parity reached. |
| Belgium (Wallonia) (BE3) | Net Billing & Green Certificates. | [CWaPE](https://www.cwape.be/) | Green certificates for production; prosumer tariff under Walloon framework; compensating counter being phased out. |
| Bulgaria (BG) | Net billing, FiTs for small & grants. | [Ministry of Energy / NRRP](https://www.me.government.bg/) | National Recovery and Resilience Plan co-finances rooftop PV grants; FiTs available for small installations (<30 kW). |
| Croatia (HR) | Net billing + fund rebates. | [FZOEU](https://www.fzoeu.hr/) | Environmental Protection and Energy Efficiency Fund (FZOEU) provides co-financing for household PV installations. |
| Cyprus (CY) | Net billing, VNM & capital subsidies. | [CERA](https://www.cera.org.cy/) | Net billing scheme; Virtual Net Metering (VNM) for energy communities; government grant programmes for residential PV. |
| Czech Republic (CZ) | Investment Subsidies. | [SFŽP / New Green Savings](https://www.sfzp.cz/) | Nová zelená úsporám (New Green Savings) programme provides grants for rooftop PV + storage on residential buildings. |
| Denmark (DK) | Net Settlement (hourly). | [Danish Energy Agency](https://ens.dk/) | Hourly net settlement since 2013; surplus exported to grid valued at hourly spot price. Local energy community frameworks. |
| Estonia (EE) | Investment subsidies. | [KredEx / KIK](https://kredex.ee/) | KredEx (now merged into Environmental Investment Centre, KIK) provides investment grants for household PV systems. |
| Finland (FI) | Investment Subsidies & Tax credits. | [Energy Authority](https://energiavirasto.fi/) | Energy Aid (Energiatuki) for SMEs; household tax deduction for installation labour costs. |
| France (FR) | Open-door FiT & Investment Premium. | [CRE / Légifrance](https://www.cre.fr/) | Fixed FiT for self-consumption with surplus sale (<100 kWp); investment premium per kWp; set by Arrêté Tarifaire, updated quarterly. |
| Germany (DE) | Fixed FiT or Sliding Premium. | [BNetzA / EEG 2023](https://www.bundesnetzagentur.de/) | EEG 2023 guarantees fixed FiT for rooftop systems <100 kWp (full feed-in or partial self-consumption rates); direct marketing option above that. |
| Greece (GR) | Net Billing & Subsidies. | [RAAEY (ex-RAE)](https://www.rae.gr/) | "Fotovoltaïká sti Stégi" (PV on the Roof) programme; transition from net metering to net billing framework completed in 2023. |
| Hungary (HU) | Gross settlement + subsidies. | [MEKH / Napenergia Plusz](https://mekh.hu/) | Napenergia Plusz grant programme for residential PV + storage. New installations post-2024 under gross settlement; grid feed-in restrictions for systems >5 kW. |
| Ireland (IE) | Clean Export Guarantee & Grants. | [CRU / SEAI](https://www.cru.ie/) | Micro-generation Support Scheme (MSS): Clean Export Guarantee tariff for exported electricity + SEAI investment grants up to €2,100. |
| Italy (IT) | Investment Tax Credits & Energy Communities. | [GSE](https://www.gse.it/) | Superbonus reduced from 110% to 70% (2024) and 65% (2025); Renewable Energy Communities (CER) incentive tariff under D.Lgs. 199/2021. |
| Latvia (LV) | Net billing + grants. | [Altum / SPRK](https://www.altum.lv/) | State development finance institution Altum provides investment grants; net billing for prosumers regulated by SPRK. |
| Lithuania (LT) | Net billing + subsidies. | [APVA / VERT](https://www.apva.lt/) | Lithuanian Environmental Investment Fund (APVA) administers household PV grants; net billing scheme since 2019 managed by VERT. |
| Luxembourg (LU) | FiT or Investment subsidies. | [ILR / Klimabonus](https://web.ilr.lu/) | Guaranteed FiT for small rooftop systems; Klimabonus programme provides additional investment subsidies for residential PV. |
| Malta (MT) | Investment grants + fixed FiT. | [REWS](https://www.rews.org.mt/) | Government grant schemes for residential PV; fixed FiT for surplus export; very high solar irradiation makes rooftop PV attractive. |
| Netherlands (NL) | Net Metering (salderingsregeling). | [RVO / Rijksoverheid](https://www.rvo.nl/) | Senate rejected the phase-out bill in February 2024; net metering scheme (salderingsregeling) remains actively in place. Future phase-out still under political discussion. |
| Norway (NO) | Plusskunde scheme + Investment Subsidies. | [Enova / NVE](https://www.enova.no/) | Plusskunde (prosumer) framework: surplus fed to grid at spot price. Enova grants for residential PV + battery investments. |
| Poland (PL) | Net Billing & Investment Subsidies. | [URE / Mój Prąd](https://mojprad.gov.pl/) | Transitioned from net metering to net billing in April 2022. Mój Prąd 5.0 (My Electricity) grant programme extended; covers PV + storage + heat pumps. |
| Portugal (PT) | Self-Consumption Framework + Grants. | [DGEG / PRR](https://www.dgeg.gov.pt/) | UPAC self-consumption framework (DL 162/2019); investment grants co-financed by PRR (Recovery and Resilience Plan). |
| Romania (RO) | Investment Subsidies & Net Billing. | [ANRE / Casa Verde](https://www.anre.ro/) | Casa Verde Fotovoltaice programme provides grants for household PV (up to 20,000 RON); net billing for prosumers under ANRE regulation. |
| Slovakia (SK) | Investment subsidies (voucher scheme). | [SIEA / Zelená domácnostiam](https://zelenadomacnostiam.sk/) | Zelená domácnostiam (Green for Households) voucher programme administered by the Slovak Innovation and Energy Agency (SIEA). |
| Slovenia (SI) | Net billing + grants. | [Borzen / Eko sklad](https://www.borzen.si/) | Net billing for prosumers; Eko sklad (Eco Fund) provides soft loans and grants for residential PV and energy renovation. |
| Spain (ES) | Investment Subsidies & Net Billing. | [IDAE / MITECO](https://www.idae.es/) | Net billing framework under RD 244/2019; investment grants co-financed by PRTR (Next Generation EU) funds via IDAE. |
| Sweden (SE) | Tax Credits/Deductions. | [Swedish Energy Agency / Skatteverket](https://www.energimyndigheten.se) | Grön teknik-avdrag (Green Technology Deduction): 20% of material and installation costs for solar PV, deducted directly on invoice. |
| Switzerland (CH) | Investment Subsidies (Einmalvergütung). | [SFOE / Pronovo](https://www.pronovo.ch/) | One-off investment contribution (Einmalvergütung, EIV) for PV systems; administered by Pronovo on behalf of the Swiss Federal Office of Energy. |
| United Kingdom (UK) | Smart Export Guarantee (SEG). | [Ofgem](https://www.ofgem.gov.uk/) | Licensed suppliers must offer SEG tariffs to small-scale generators (<5 MW); rate is market-based (not guaranteed minimum). FiT scheme closed to new applicants Jan 2020. |

| Portugal (PT) | CfDs via tenders. | [DGEG](https://www.dgeg.gov.pt/) | DGEG tenders for onshore wind; CfD framework; legacy FiT contracts still running for older parks. |
| Romania (RO) | Two-way CfDs. | [ANRE](https://www.anre.ro/) | CfD framework co-financed by Modernisation Fund; Romania has significant onshore wind capacity (mainly Dobrogea region). |
| Slovakia (SK) | Feed-in Premium. | [ÚRSO](https://www.urso.gov.sk/) | Very limited onshore wind deployment; feed-in premium available in principle. |
| Slovenia (SI) | Market Premium via tenders. | [Borzen](https://www.borzen.si/) | Borzen RES support scheme; very limited onshore wind capacity in Slovenia. |
| Spain (ES) | REER Pay-as-bid tenders. | [MITECO](https://www.miteco.gob.es/) | REER competitive tenders; Spain is one of the largest onshore wind markets in Europe. |
| Sweden (SE) | Merchant / Corporate PPAs (Elcertifikat phased out). | [Swedish Energy Agency](https://www.energimyndigheten.se) | Elcertifikat closed to new entrants; onshore wind expansion driven entirely by corporate PPAs and merchant revenue. |
| Switzerland (CH) | Market Premium. | [SFOE / Pronovo](https://www.pronovo.ch/) | Feed-in premium under the Energy Act; very limited onshore wind deployment due to permitting and local opposition. |
| United Kingdom (UK) | Contracts for Difference (CfD). | [LCCC / DESNZ](https://www.lowcarboncontracts.uk/) | CfD Allocation Rounds; onshore wind re-included in CfD auctions from AR5 (2023) after years of exclusion. |

## CO2 Incentives / Quotas / Taxes

| Country | Mechanism | Source / Reference | Comment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
